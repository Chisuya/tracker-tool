Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2025

TL;DR: All your data stays on your computer. I don't see or collect anything. The app only talks to Google Calendar (if you choose to use that feature).

What Data Does Time Tool Store?

Time Tool stores everything locally on your computer in a SQLite database:

  • Which apps you use and for how long
  • Your time tracking sessions and idle periods
  • Your project names and settings
  • Your theme preferences

None of this data is sent anywhere. It stays on your computer.

Google Calendar Access

If you choose to sync with Google Calendar, Time Tool will:

  • Create calendar events showing your tracked time
  • Use colour-coding to identify different apps
  • Access your Google Calendar (obviously) and basic profile info for authentication

You can revoke this access anytime through your Google Account settings.

Time Tool's use of Google Calendar follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.

What I Don't Do

  • I don't collect or see your data
  • I don't use analytics or tracking
  • I don't share anything with anyone
  • I don't have any servers - there's nowhere for your data to go!

Your Data, Your Control

Want to delete everything? Just uninstall the app and delete its folder. Want to export your data? It's in a standard SQLite database you can open with any SQLite viewer.
